## Overview

Grammarly Professional Suite is a complete AI writing platform that serves professionals and teams with style enforcement, brand voice consistency, and workflow integration needed for organizational writing quality at scale.

Style guide enforcement applies organization-specific writing rules including preferred terminology, banned words, capitalization conventions, and formatting standards across every team member's writing. Custom rules ensure brand names and product terminology are used consistently.

Grammarly Business analytics provide visibility into team writing patterns, common error types, and improvement trends over time. Aggregate statistics show which issues are most prevalent, and individual reports help team members focus on growth areas.

Full-sentence rewrites powered by generative AI suggest alternative phrasings for entire sentences that improve clarity, impact, or tone while preserving the original meaning. The rewrite suggestions offer multiple alternatives with different emphases.

Grammarly Professional Suite is the writing platform for organizations that recognize clear communication as a competitive advantage and want AI-powered tools that ensure every piece of communication meets professional standards.

## Performance & Integration

Grammarly is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Grammarly processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
